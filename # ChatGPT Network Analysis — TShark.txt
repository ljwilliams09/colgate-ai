## Data Collected
- 3 sessions captured: page load (init), simple prompt ("hey there"), complex task
- Tool: TShark on Windows Wi-Fi interface, filtering TLS handshakes + packet sizes
 
## Traffic Volume
- A simple "hey there" prompt transferred **1.88 MB** of data
- Page load alone used **1.08 MB** before any prompt was sent
- A complex task prompt ran for **104 seconds** of sustained network activity
- You sent **more data than you received** in every session
 
## Third-Party Domains Contacted
- **Google Analytics** is pinged on every prompt, not just on page load
- **Datadog** (error monitoring) connects during active chat sessions
- **Cloudflare telemetry** runs silently in the background
- **Intercom** (support chat widget) opens a live WebSocket connection on init
- Total of **6 third-party services** detected across sessions
 
## Privacy
- Google Analytics receives data every time you send a message to ChatGPT
- Third-party connections happen even during a simple one-word prompt
- Your conversation activity is observable by at least 3 external companies
